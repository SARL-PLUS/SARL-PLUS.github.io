Research Areas

Our team focuses on these key areas of reinforcement learning and AI research:

Multivariate Time Series Analysis

Developing novel approaches for analyzing complex temporal data patterns and trends across multiple variables.

Bayesian Methods

Applying probabilistic modeling and Bayesian inference to handle uncertainty and incorporate prior knowledge in learning systems.

Human-AI Integration

Creating synergistic systems that combine human expertise with artificial intelligence capabilities for optimal decision-making.

Announcements & Upcoming Events

Featured

Reinforcement Learning Bootcamp 2025

September 17-19, 2025 Salzburg, Austria

Exciting News: We're thrilled to announce that the Bootcamp will once again take place in Salzburg from September 17th to 19th this year!

The SARL team is organizing an intensive bootcamp focused on Reinforcement Learning, designed for students, researchers, and industry professionals interested in learning and applying RL techniques.

What to Expect

  • Hands-on Learning: Practice implementing RL algorithms
  • Expert Guidance: Learn from leading researchers in the field
  • Collaborative Environment: Work on projects with peers
  • Networking Opportunities: Connect with the international RL community

Past Success

Our previous bootcamp transformed from a small student event into an international gathering with participants from around the world. It was a collaborative effort with partners from Fraunhofer Austria, JOANNEUM RESEARCH ROBOTICS, AI Austria, ExtensityAI, and our SARL team from IDA Lab Salzburg.
